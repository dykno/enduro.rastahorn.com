Details

We will be hosting 3 stops in the series: Scappoose, Cold Creek, and Post Canyon. Each stop will have a designated day that we will race as a group; however, you will also be able to race the segments on your own at anytime up to 5 days prior to the designated race day.

You are allowed one shuttle ride to the top with any subsequent sections being pedaled. Segments must be raced in the order given on this website or by an organizer.

There will be winners for each stop as well as an overall winner for the whole series. You don't win shit.

There is a $20 entrance fee for the whole series. This fee covers the costs of t-shirts with any left over funds being donated to {INSERT_CHARITY_HERE}.
